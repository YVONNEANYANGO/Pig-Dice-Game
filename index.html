Pig-Dice-Game

Background Information about the Pig-Dice-Game

Pig-Dice is a simple and interesting game played mostly by children in South America and other parts of the world. The other name for the game is Ambition.The game can be played by two users playing agains each other.

Pig Dice Variants

Just like any other game pig dice also has several variants.The following represents the three types of variants

  • Two Dice Pig: When a player rolls two dice and one of the die is a 1, she loses the points just for that turn. If she rolls double 1s, she loses his total score!
  • Big Pig: When a player rolls two dice and one of the die is a 1, she loses the points just for that turn. If she rolls double 1s, she adds 25 to her score, but must roll again!
  • Big Pig variation: When a player rolls two dice and one of the die is a 1, she loses the points just for that turn. If she rolls double 1s, she adds 25 to her score. If she rolls any other doubles, she adds a double score to her total. After rolling a double, player must roll again.

Pig dice players can either choose to play either the easy or hard levels.

The rules of the Game

  • First player rolls the die as many times as he or she wants provided the die doesnt roll a 1
  • First player continue to accumulate points but once the die rolls a 1 the player looses the points he or she accumulated in that turn and passes over to the second player
  • The play goes round untill a winner is determined
  • The first player to score 100 points and above wins the game!!!
